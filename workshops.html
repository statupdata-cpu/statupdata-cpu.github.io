Workshop 2 — SPSS Hands-on: Basics → Intermediate

Overview: A 3-day hands-on workshop introducing SPSS for statistical analysis. Topics include data import, cleaning, descriptive statistics, hypothesis testing, regression, and reporting. Sessions are practical, using real datasets and step-by-step demonstrations for beginners and intermediate learners. By the end, participants will confidently prepare data, run essential tests, interpret results, and generate research-ready reports.

Course structure

  • Day 1: Data handling, import, cleaning, descriptive statistics and basic charts.
  • Day 2: Hypothesis testing (t-tests, ANOVA), non-parametric tests and interpretation.
  • Day 3: Regression (linear, logistic basics), reporting outputs and export of results.
